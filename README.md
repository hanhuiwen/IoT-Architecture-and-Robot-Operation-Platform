# Intelligent Robotic Operating Platforms
## An IoT-Driven Architecture Integrating LLMs and Generative AI

---

### Abstract / 摘要

#### English Version
**Abstract**— This paper introduces an innovative design for robotic operating platforms, underpinned by a transformative Internet of Things (IoT) architecture that seamlessly integrates cutting-edge technologies such as **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Generative AI**, **Edge Computing**, and **5G networks**. The proposed platform aims to elevate the intelligence and autonomy of IoT systems and robotics, enabling them to perform real-time decision-making and adapt dynamically to shifting environments. 

Through a series of compelling case studies across sectors—including **smart manufacturing**, **healthcare**, and the **service industry**—this paper demonstrates the substantial potential of IoT-enabled robotics to optimize operational workflows, enhance productivity, and deliver innovative, scalable solutions. By emphasizing the roles of LLMs and Generative AI, the research highlights how these technologies drive the evolution of intelligent robotics and IoT, shaping the future of industry-specific advancements. The findings not only showcase the transformative power of these technologies but also offer a forward-looking perspective on their broader societal and industrial implications, positioning them as catalysts for next-generation automation and technological convergence.

---

#### 中文
**摘要**— 本文介绍了一种创新的机器人操作平台设计，该设计以变革性的物联网 (IoT) 架构为支撑，无缝集成了**大语言模型 (LLMs)**、**生成式 AI**、**边缘计算**和 **5G 网络**等前沿技术。所提出的平台旨在提升物联网系统与机器人的智能水平和自主性，使它们能够进行实时决策并动态适应不断变化的环境。

通过对**智能制造**、**医疗保健**和**服务业**等行业的一系列典型案例研究，本文展示了赋能物联网的机器人在优化操作工作流、提高生产力以及交付创新且具扩展性方案方面的巨大潜力。通过强调 LLMs 和生成式 AI 的作用，本研究突出了这些技术如何驱动智能机器人与物联网的演进，并塑造特定行业进步的未来。研究结果不仅展示了这些技术的变革力量，还对其更广泛的社会和工业影响提出了前瞻性见解，将其定位为下一代自动化与技术融合的催化剂。
